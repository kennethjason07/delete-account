Account Deletion – VidyaSetu

VidyaSetu allows users to request deletion of their account.

To delete your account:

  1. Send an email to support@vidyasetu.app
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email or phone number

Once verified, your account will be permanently deleted within 7 working days.

Some data may be retained if required by law.
